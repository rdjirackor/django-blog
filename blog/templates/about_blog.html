About the Blog

Hi, you found it — welcome.

I began this blog because thoughts do not remain quietly in my mind and posting here allows me to catch them, wrestle them onto paper, and sometimes — occasionally — shape them into something worth reading.

Here are ideas that likely shouldn't work, but might. Here are the deep dives, the rants, the overthinking, and the random flashes of brilliance that occur at 2 AM when regular humans are dreaming.

This is a blog for the curious, a blog for the ones who think in "what ifs" and "why nots" instead of "maybes." It's uncurated, just me expressing anything I can think of and it's definitely not polished. It's not sanitized. It's alive. It's the inside of my brain with no filter.

So if you ever imagined that your brain is like a browser with 84 open tabs — and 7 of them are blasting music — then you'll probably feel right at home here.

Thanks for dropping by, I hope you stay and stay curious.
